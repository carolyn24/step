My Travel Experiences

Another thing I love to do is travel. I love meeting new people, learning about their cultures, trying new foods, and also collecting souvenirs from all the places I have been.


I love to photograph my experiences of the places that I visit, as a way of keeping the experience with me.

California

I came to California for the first time in 2019, when I first moved to the US to go to Stanford.

There are may things I love about Palo Alto, but my favorite thing is when the entirety of University Avenue is decorated for the festive season in the winter.

Other places I have visited in California which I loved are the Golden Gate Bridge in San Francisco and Pier 39
